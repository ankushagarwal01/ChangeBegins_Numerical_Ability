# New and Modified Numerical Templates

This document lists only the templates that were **added** (new content) or **modified** (difficulty level changed). Kept templates are unchanged and retain their original IDs, so they are not listed here.

Each entry shows the new template ID, the template name, and what was added or changed.

---

## New templates (new content)

### NUM_RP_L3_095 — Ratio Change Over Time
New L3. Two ratio states linked by a fixed change; solved by simultaneous equations.

### NUM_RP_L3_096 — Investment Ratio With Mid-Period Change
New L3. Time-weighted (capital-months) ratio differs from the stated ratio when a partner changes capital mid-period.

### NUM_RP_L3_097 — Reverse Ratio With Replacement
New L3. Work backwards from a known final ratio to the unknown amount of mixture removed.

### NUM_PC_L3_098 — Successive Percentage Changes
New L3. Successive percentage changes compounded on a running base; the additive answer is the trap.

### NUM_PC_L3_099 — Reverse-Engineer Original From a Multi-Variable Outcome
New L3. Reverse-engineer a base from percentages-of-percentages and one absolute margin.

### NUM_PC_L3_100 — Successive Discounts and Profit
New L3. Markup then two successive discounts; profit measured on cost, discounts multiplicative not additive.

### NUM_PL_L3_102 — Chain of Transactions
New L3. Chain of sales (A→B→C) resolved backwards through two successive margins.

### NUM_PL_L3_103 — False Weight With Markup
New L3. False weight and price markup combine multiplicatively into the true profit.

### NUM_AV_L3_106 — Overlapping Subgroups
New L3. Overall average plus two overlapping subgroup averages; find the remaining group's average via totals.

### NUM_AV_L3_107 — Average Shift (Batting Average)
New L3. Average before and after extra items; find the subgroup average using totals, not by subtracting averages.

### NUM_AV_L3_108 — Unknown Group Size
New L3. The group size is the unknown; set up and solve a weighted-average equation for the count.

### NUM_MA_L3_110 — Three Sources, Equal Amounts
New L3. Equal amounts of several sources mixed; convert ratios to fractions and combine.

### NUM_MA_L3_111 — Add Pure Substance
New L3. Add a pure substance to hit a target concentration; numerator and denominator both change.

### NUM_MA_L3_112 — Reverse Mixture
New L3. Reverse mixture: recover source quantities from a known final volume and concentration (two equations).

### NUM_SI_L3_114 — Doubling / Tripling Time
New L3. Derive the rate from a doubling time, then solve for the time to reach another multiple (SI).

### NUM_CI_L3_115 — CI − SI Difference
New L3. Use the closed form CI−SI = P(R/100)^2 for two years to solve backwards for the principal.

### NUM_TW_L3_117 — Three-Equation Rate System
New L3. Solve a three-equation system from pairwise work rates (A+B, B+C, A+C).

### NUM_TSD_L3_118 — Boats and Streams System
New L3. Boats-and-streams: two trips give two equations for still-water and stream speed.

### NUM_PT_L3_120 — Proportional-Rate Pipes
New L3. Pipes with proportional rates (r, 2r, 4r); solve from the combined fill time.

### NUM_PN_L3_122 — Divisibility Arrangement
New L3. Count k-digit numbers meeting a divisibility rule: select valid digit sets, then permute.

### NUM_PN_L3_123 — Circular With Restriction
New L3. Circular arrangement with a together/apart restriction using the (n-1)! base.

### NUM_PR_L3_124 — Conditional / Bayes
New L3. Conditional/Bayes posterior from base rates and conditional rates. Filled a gap (no conditional probability existed).

### NUM_PR_L3_125 — Conditional on an Event
New L3. Conditional probability by restricting the sample space to a stated event.

### NUM_DV_L3_127 — HCF–LCM Product Relation
New L3. Use the product = HCF x LCM relation to find a missing number.

### NUM_DV_L3_128 — Common-Remainder Problem
New L3. Smallest number leaving a fixed remainder under several divisors (LCM plus offset).

### NUM_RM_L3_129 — Large-Power Remainder
New L3. Large-power remainder by reducing the exponent via the cycle mod m.

### NUM_RM_L3_130 — Last Two Digits
New L3. Last two digits of a large power via the cycle mod 100.

### NUM_MS2_L3_132 — Path Around a Rectangle
New L3. Area of a uniform-width path around a rectangle (outer minus inner).

### NUM_MS2_L3_133 — Wire Reshaped
New L3. A wire of fixed length reshaped between two figures; compare areas.

### NUM_MS3_L3_135 — Melting and Recasting
New L3. Melting and recasting a solid into smaller ones by volume conservation.

### NUM_MS3_L3_136 — Painted Cube
New L3. Painted-cube: classify unit cubes by number of painted faces.

## Modified templates (difficulty level changed)

These templates existed before but had their difficulty level changed. Under the numbering rules they are treated as new and receive new IDs (95+); their previous numbers are retired.

### NUM_PL_L2_101 — Same Selling Price, One Profit One Loss

### NUM_AV_L2_104 — Reverse Average (Missing Value)
Modified: reclassified L3 -> L2. Reverse average (find the missing value for a target mean); the old bound was synthetic and dropped.

### NUM_AV_L2_105 — Reverse Weighted Average
Modified: reclassified L3 -> L2. Reverse weighted average (required subgroup change); the old bound was synthetic and dropped.

### NUM_MA_L2_109 — Multi-Step Replacement
Modified: reclassified L3 -> L2. Multi-step replacement c x (1-f)^n; the old feasibility check was synthetic and dropped.

### NUM_CI_L2_113 — Compare Two Plans

### NUM_TW_L2_116 — Efficiency Multiplier

### NUM_PT_L2_119 — Three-Phase Fill

### NUM_PN_L2_121 — Multi-Constraint Counting (Gap Method)

### NUM_NS_L2_126 — Rule Identification

### NUM_MS2_L2_131 — Cost With Wastage

### NUM_MS3_L2_134 — Capacity With Efficiency
Modified: reclassified L3 -> L2. Capacity with efficiency; one modifier and a comparison is L2.
