Question 2: A factory can produce between 200 and 300 units of a product per day. If the factory operates 5 days a week, what is the maximum number of units it can produce in a week?

Options:
A) 1,000
B) 1,500
C) 1,200
D) 1,800

Correct Answer: D
Explanation: The maximum weekly production is calculated by multiplying the maximum daily production by the number of days: 300 units/day * 5 days = 1,500 units.
